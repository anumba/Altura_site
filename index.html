Featured Work

Recent Implementation

Custom Software Education

Enterprise Student Management Portal

The Problem

A growing institution managed student records through disconnected spreadsheets. Administrative staff spent hours on routine data entry, and parents had no access to progress information.

The Approach

A centralized web application designed with role-based access for administrators, teachers, students, and parents. System architecture prioritized automation of the most time-intensive processes first.

The Solution

A fully functional student management system with automated attendance tracking, grade calculation, parent portal access, and administrative dashboards for enrollment management.

The Outcome

Administrative workload reduced 60% for routine tasks. Report generation dropped from days to under 10 minutes. System in continuous operation for over two years at 99.8% uptime.
